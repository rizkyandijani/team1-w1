What is sim racing?


Short for “Simulation Racing”, it is a computer simulation designed to replicate real life racing as close as possible. Sim racing gives you accurate physics, realistic tire wear and traction limits, fully adjustable suspension, laser scanned circuits, and beautifully modelled cars. Sim Racing is different than a racing video game, which any novice can drive. In sim racing , the skills and techniques are the same as in a real car. In fact, many race car drivers use sims for training.

Our Facilities

Our spacious facility is located in the middle of South Jakarta. We have 4 sim-racing rigs available at our space, all built with high quality components. Cockpits are made by HM Engineering, fully adjustable, and built in Indonesia. The wheel, pedals, and shifter are all from Fanatec; a brand used by serious sim racers around the world. On each of our rigs, you have the option of playing either on triple monitors or in VR (virtual reality) for the most immersive experience.



The Rig

Each of our 4 rigs will have the following parts:

Cockpit: HM Engineering GT-One
Monitors: Triple 27" Monitors, Running at 5760x1080 Resolution
VR Headset: Pimax 5K Plus Headset
Wheel: Fanatec Clubsport V2.5 Racing Wheel
Pedals: Fanatec Clubsport Pedals USA
Shifter: Fanatec ClubSport Static Shifter Paddles & ClubSport Shifter SQ V1.5
Handbrake: DSD Hydraulic Handbrake
