Our Facilities

We have 4 sim-racing rigs available at our space, built with high quality components. You have the option of playing either on triple monitors or in VR (virtual reality).


The Rig

Cockpit : HM Engineering GT-One
Monitors : Triple 27" monitors, running at 5760x1080 resolution
VR Headset: Pimax 5K plus headset
Wheel : Fanatec Clubsport V2.5 Racing Wheel
Pedals : Fanatec Clubsport Pedals USA
Shifter : Fanatec ClubSport Static Shifter Paddles & ClubSport Shifter SQ V1.5


Choice of Simulators

Each rig uses a PC equipped with a high end GPU. Every PC will have all the following simulators installed:

  • iRacing
  • Rfactor2
  • Assetto Corsa
  • Project Cars 2
  • F1 2018
  • Dirt Rally
